About Éclair & Crumb

Slow baking, warm hospitality.

We are a small neighbourhood pastry cafe in Indiranagar, Bangalore, inspired by classic French techniques and Indian flavours.

Our Story

From early-morning dough to evening chai.

Every batch begins long before sunrise, with butter, flour, and a lot of patience.

Éclair & Crumb started as a home oven and a notebook of family recipes. What began as weekend bakes for friends slowly turned into a tiny pastry counter, then a cozy cafe on a quiet Indiranagar street.

Our menu is intentionally small, so we can give each piece the time it deserves. You will find classic viennoiserie alongside flavours like cardamom, saffron, jaggery, and filter coffee — all made in small batches, with ingredients we are proud to list out loud.

Meet the Baker

The hands behind every layer.

Head baker Ananya spent years working in Bangalore kitchens before training in Paris, and eventually returning home to build a cafe that feels like a living room.

What she believes in

Butter over shortcuts, seasonal fruit over food colouring, and coffee that is always freshly ground.

In the kitchen by

4:30am most mornings, laminating dough while the city is still quiet.
